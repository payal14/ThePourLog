Why a dial, not a star rating

Five stars tells you nothing about a coffee tool — a kettle can be a "5" for control and a "2" for value. So instead we score every piece of gear on where it sits between "entry-level" and "obsessive," the same way you'd read a roast level. Light means beginner-friendly and forgiving. Dark means built for people who already own a refractometer.

This site earns a small commission when you buy through our links, at no extra cost to you — that's what funds the coffee (and the gear) that makes these reviews possible.
